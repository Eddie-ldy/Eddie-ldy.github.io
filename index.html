About

Dan-Ying Liu is currently studying in Nanjing University and majoring in journalism and communication. She is  the Associate Director of the New Media Center in the Student Affairs Office of Nanjing University and an advisor to the Nanjing University Tianjian Association. In the meantime, she hopes to pursue a career related to radio and television after graduating from college.
